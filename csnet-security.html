Who owns the data stored in CSnet®?
▾

All data entered into CSnet systems remains the property of your organisation. Full stop.

CSnet® does not claim ownership of client or organisational data and only uses it to deliver and support the CSnet® service in accordance with our licence agreement.

✅

Your data belongs to you — always. CSnet is the custodian, not the owner.

🇦🇺
Where is CSnet® data hosted?
▾

All CSnet® customer data is hosted and stored entirely within Australia.

The platform is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the Asia Pacific (Sydney) region, using multiple availability zones for resilience and protection against localised outages.

📍

AWS Asia Pacific (Sydney) — multiple availability zones for maximum resilience.

⚖️
Does CSnet® comply with Australian privacy laws?
▾

Yes. CSnet® is an Australian company and complies with:

The Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
The Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) Scheme
Relevant state-based privacy and information protection laws

CSnet works in partnership with organisations to meet these shared legal obligations.

🛡️
How is our data protected?
▾

CSnet® uses multiple layers of security controls, including:

Encryption of all data at rest
Strict access controls with identity management and multi-factor authentication
Real-time monitoring of the hosting environment
Regular security reviews and independent testing

Security protections are applied across infrastructure, systems, and processes.

How often is data backed up?
▾

Each CSnet® organisation database is backed up every five minutes, allowing point-in-time recovery within minutes if required.

⏱️

5-minute backup intervals mean your data is never more than minutes away from full recovery.

🔄
What happens if there is a system outage or disaster?
▾

CSnet® has documented and tested business continuity and disaster recovery processes in place. If a serious system failure occurs:

Services can be restored in alternate Australian availability zones
Customer data can be recovered to within minutes of the last backup
Full disaster recovery rehearsals are conducted annually to validate these processes

Customers are kept informed in line with CSnet® communication procedures and service commitments.
